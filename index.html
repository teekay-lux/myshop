About Teekay Lux

Founded in 2023, Teekay Lux represents the pinnacle of watchmaking craftsmanship. Each timepiece is a testament to our dedication to quality, precision, and timeless design.

Our watches are more than just instruments to tell time - they are heirlooms, statements of personal style, and symbols of achievement.

We source only the finest materials and employ master watchmakers to create pieces that will be treasured for generations.
